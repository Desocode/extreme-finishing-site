Intro

Extreme Finishing is an automotive bodyshop located in Basingstoke. We are specialised in light accident repair and show quality paintwork.

Jamie has been in the business for over 15 years and his passion for cars and auto body work is unparalled. His attention to detail, eye for design and commitment to excellence has made him well respected and sought after in the automotive industry. From small to large-scale projects, Jamie has the expertise and skill to take a vehicle to better than showroom condition.

Check out some of the awesome work done at Extreme Finishing.

About

At Extreme Finishing, we offer a range of services to bring your car back to its former glory. We specialize in light body repairs, show quality paintwork, dent removal, rust repair and more. We can also provide custom designs and modifications for your vehicle.

If you would like to find out more about our services, please contact us via phone or email.
